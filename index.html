About Me.

As a passionate Special Adviser for Science and Technology and the Co-ordinator of the Youth Assembly in Lagos, Nigeria, I am dedicated to empowering the next generation through education and innovation. With a strong belief in the transformative power of knowledge, I strive to create opportunities that inspire young minds to pursue academic excellence and embrace the wonders of science and technology. My journey in this field has been driven by a commitment to fostering a culture of curiosity and critical thinking among our youth. I work tirelessly to develop programs and initiatives that not only enhance educational access but also encourage hands-on learning and practical application of scientific principles. By collaborating with schools, universities, and industry leaders, I aim to bridge the gap between education and real-world challenges, equipping our youth with the skills they need to thrive in an ever-evolving global landscape. I am particularly passionate about promoting STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics) education, as I believe it is essential for nurturing innovative thinkers and problem solvers. Through workshops, mentorship programs, and community outreach, I seek to ignite a passion for learning and exploration in young people, helping them to realize their potential and contribute meaningfully to society. In my role, I also advocate for policies that support educational reform and investment in technology, ensuring that our youth have access to the resources and opportunities necessary for success. Together, we can build a brighter future for Nigeria, where every young person is empowered to dream big and achieve their goals. I am excited about the journey ahead and look forward to collaborating with like-minded individuals and organizations to make a lasting impact in the lives of our youth.
